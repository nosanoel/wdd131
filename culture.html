Nigerian Culture

Nigeria’s culture is a vibrant mix of music, dance, festivals, and traditions. Afrobeat music, pioneered by Fela Kuti, continues to influence global sound.

Fashion in Nigeria is bold and colorful, with Ankara and Aso-ebi fabrics widely celebrated. Festivals like the Osun-Osogbo and Durbar showcase the nation's cultural richness.

Explore Nigeria – Website Plan

Site Name

Explore Nigeria

This name captures the essence of the site’s mission: to showcase Nigeria’s rich culture, history, and tourist attractions. It’s simple, memorable, and directly communicates the purpose of the site.

Optional domain availability: explorenigeria.org

Site Purpose

The site aims to promote tourism and cultural awareness in Nigeria. It will provide information on historical landmarks, cultural festivals, travel tips, and local cuisine. The site will also feature a contact form for inquiries and suggestions.

Scenarios

  • What are the top tourist destinations in Nigeria?
  • How can I learn more about Nigerian cultural festivals?

Color Schema

  • Turquoise (#00CED1): Used for headings and accents to reflect vibrancy and energy.
  • Black (#000000): Used for background to create contrast and highlight content.
  • Light Gray (#f0f0f0): Used for body text to ensure readability against the dark background.

Typography

  • Segoe UI: Used for all headings and body text for a clean, modern look.
  • Monospace: Used for wireframe sketches and code-style elements.

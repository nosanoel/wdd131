Explore Nigeria - Website Planning Document

Site Name

Site Name: Explore Nigeria

This name was chosen because the website is focused on introducing people to Nigeria's rich culture, Afrobeat music, and beautiful travel destinations. It invites visitors to explore everything Nigeria has to offer.


Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate and inspire visitors by showcasing the diverse aspects of Nigerian culture. It will provide:

  • Informative content on Nigerian traditions, food, languages, and fashion
  • Insights into Afrobeat musicâ€”its roots and modern artists
  • Travel guides for exploring Nigeria s tourist destinations

Scenarios

  • What are the top cultural festivals in Nigeria that I can attend as a tourist?
  • Who are the most influential Afrobeat artists today, and where can I listen to their music?

Color Schema

  • Green (#008000): Used for main headings, buttons, and navigation bar. Symbolizes Nigeria's national color.
  • Gold (#FFD700): Used for highlights, icons, and call-to-action elements. Represents richness and vibrancy.

Typography

  • Font: Poppins (Google Font)
  • Usage: Used for all headings and body text to maintain consistency and readability across devices.
